Eastern Music Festival

Amazing musical performances from famous artists

Event Information

Date: March 15, 2024

Time: 7:00 PM

Location: City Theater

Category: Music

Event Description

We are pleased to invite you to attend the Eastern Music Festival that brings together the best local and Arab artists. You will enjoy a wonderful musical evening full of beautiful melodies and outstanding performances.

The festival will include:

  • Various musical performances from Arabic heritage
  • Live performances by famous artists
  • Audience participation in some songs
  • Advanced light show accompanying the music
